About the hyneks

The parents:
Mark & Lynnette Hynek

They lived very different lives.
Mark lived a worldly life and began his journey with Christ when he was in his thirties.
Lynnette grew up in a God fearing family and knew Christ most of her life.
They got married and had three children then God led them to the decision to be missionaries to Costa Rica and they had their fourth child there.

The children:
Elizabeth, Elysia, Jonathan, & Jared

Elizabeth, the first born, was a very social child and thrived in the culture of Costa Rica. She decided to pursue a major that would teach her how to help children that have been abused.
Elysia, second child, the evil mastermind. always thinking of how to prank her friends. She decided to pursue a career in law enforcement.
Jonathan, the third, anti-social and has a dark imagination. Loves spending time with friends but also spending time alone for hours. Decided to pursue a major in filming.
Jared, final child, an artist with paper and baked goods. Could play video games the whole day if allowed to. Wants to create stories in a comic format.
